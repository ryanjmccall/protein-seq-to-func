CCR7

Overview

CCR7 is a chemokine receptor involved in the migration of immune cells, particularly T cells and dendritic cells, to secondary lymphoid organs. It plays a critical role in adaptive immune responses and immune surveillance. While CCR7 is not directly implicated in aging or longevity in the provided data, its function in immune system regulation may indirectly influence healthspan and longevity through maintenance of immune homeostasis.
